Why is it necessary?

Often is kinda hard to mantain motivation while learning a language, and sometimes one is just bored or tired of studying, so it's helpful to make it feel like a game, because, it's hard to get bored of playing a game, right? That's why apps like Duolingo or Memrise are so popular, because of gamification.
So that's what this aims to do, gamify immersion to make it more like a game, leveling up your skills and competing with others in the leaderboard.
