Features

Clipboard page

A clipboard page with customization options and that is able to save your progress in a database so it is accessible anywhere.

Xp and level system

It will have an Xp and level system in which you can level up different skills (Reading, listening, etc...) by immersing.

Recommendations

It will recommend you content by your level, things you've consumed before and your current level of understanding, so the content is the most comprehensible and it has the most i+1 sentences.

Leaderboard

A leaderboard can be motivational and it promoves a healthy competitiveness that way everyone strives to be in the top 10 motivating them to do more immersion.

Detailed statistics

A detailed inform of your past week immersion with graphs and comparations to your other weeks, how much new vocab you mined, how much did your understanding improve and comparisons with your previous weeks.

Anki integration

Anki is the best SRS tool and is open source, and it will have Anki integration allowing it to automatically update your stats according to the states of your cards or how many you mined.
